## The Magic Bean

Deep in the soil of Gielinor, something stirs.

Farmers whisper of a rare, unusual seed -- unlike any other. It has been known to appear, once in a great while, as a surprise among a harvest. Some say it responds to the earth differently. Some say it grows... impossibly tall.

Its true nature is unclear. Those who have found one speak only in riddles.

> "I planted it and waited. I am still waiting."
> "The notes it left me were unsettling."
> "I climbed. I will say no more."
