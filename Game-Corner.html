Game Corner

Visit the Game Corner in Town to try your luck at eight casino-style mini-games. All games cost coins to play and pay out coins on a win. Every win also has a 1-in-1,000 chance to drop a Game Corner exclusive item.

These items can only be obtained from the Game Corner.

High-Low

Spend a bet to roll a die (1–6). Guess whether the next roll will be Higher or Lower.

  • Win — pot grows to 1.8× its current value
  • Tie — push; pot is returned
  • Lose — pot is lost

After a win you can Roll Again (the full pot rides on the next guess) or Cash Out to bank your winnings.

Lottery

Buy tickets for 1,000 coins each (up to 20 held). A draw becomes available every 24 hours.

Each ticket is resolved independently:

Chance Prize

Card Flip

Three cards are placed face-down; one is the Ace. Pick a card — if it is the Ace you win 2.5× your bet. The other two cards are revealed after your pick.

Blackjack

Standard Blackjack rules. The dealer hits on 16 or lower and stands on 17 or higher.

Outcome Payout
Blackjack (A + 10-value on deal) 2.5×
Win 2×
Push Bet returned
Lose / Bust Bet lost

Double Down is available on your opening two cards if you can afford to match your current bet.
